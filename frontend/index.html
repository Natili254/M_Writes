About Me

"I am Mutashi — a poet, a thinker, a feeler of deep things"

I write to make sense of the world and the emotions that move through it.

Through words, he explores love, healing, identity, and the beauty of becoming. His poetry is a sanctuary — a place where vulnerability is strength and every feeling has a voice. Each piece is an invitation to pause, reflect, and reconnect with the parts of ourselves we often leave unspoken.
